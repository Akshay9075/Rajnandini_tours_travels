Harihareshwar, a small coastal town in Raigad district Maharashtra state. It is situated on the shores of the Arabian Sea and surrounded by three hills named Harihareshwar, Harshinachal, and Pushpadri. The river Savitri arrives into the Arabian Sea from the town of Harihareshwar. Lord Harihareshwar’s temple is situated in the northern side of the town. Thus, Harihareshwar is often referred to as Dev-Ghar or "House of God".
