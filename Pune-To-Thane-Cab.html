Explore the Charm of Thane with Rajnandini Tours & Travels: Your Trusted Pune to Thane Cab Service. Situated just 155 kilometers away from Pune, Thane is a bustling city in Maharashtra known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and scenic beauty. Embark on a comfortable and convenient journey with us to discover why Thane is worth visiting.

Discovering Thane: A Glimpse into Maharashtra's Cultural Tapestry
  • Historic Landmarks: Explore the iconic Upvan Lake dating back to the 10th century, surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Cultural Heritage: Visit the Kopineshwar Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, renowned for its architectural beauty.
  • Natural Beauty: Discover Sanjay Gandhi National Park and the ancient Kanheri Caves with rock-cut sculptures.
  • Shopping and Dining: Indulge in vibrant markets and savor delectable street food at numerous eateries.
  • Adventure Activities: Experience trekking, hiking, and boat rides along the Ulhas River.
Top Places to Visit near Thane
  • Elephanta Caves: UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient rock-cut temples.
  • Powai Lake: Scenic artificial lake offering boating and picnicking opportunities.
  • Tikuji-Ni-Wadi: Popular amusement park with thrilling rides and water slides.
  • Naneghat Hills: Trek through the Western Ghats with panoramic views.
  • Kelva Beach: Pristine shores with golden sands and tranquil ambiance.
